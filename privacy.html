This Privacy Policy applies to Crazy Pop Runner ("the Game"), developed by Aaqib Javed. We are committed to transparency about how we treat your information. By downloading, installing, or playing the Game, you agree to the terms described here.

No Data Collection

The developer does not collect any personal or anonymous data from users of Crazy Pop Runner. We do not gather, transmit, or sell:

  • Names, email addresses, or other contact information

Your play stays on your device. We have no servers that store your information.

Progress, settings, and preferences (e.g. sound on/off, unlocked levels) are stored locally on your device. This data is not synced to the cloud or sent to us or any third party. Removing the Game from your device will delete this local data.
