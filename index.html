Crazy Pop Runner

Pop Balloons · Escape Enemies · Beat the Clock!

🆓 Free 🎮 3D Runner 📱 iPhone & iPad 4+

Run through exciting 3D paths, pop every balloon, and reach the finish before time runs out. Crazy Pop Runner is a fast-paced 3D runner where you guide your character through winding paths filled with colorful balloons. Pop them all and cross the line in time—but watch out: some levels have enemies chasing you! Use power-ups like Shield and Speed Runner to stay ahead. Each level gets tougher with more balloons and trickier paths. Can you pop them all and escape in time?
